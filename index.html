Fake Message Detection

Protect yourself from fake messages, scams, phishing, hacking attempts and malicious links.

What is this site?
This tool helps you quickly check text messages or chat content for signs of scams. We look for suspicious keywords, dangerous links, urgent requests (like OTP or password), and QR / fake link warnings — then give a simple Safe or Unsafe result.
Why use it?
Scammers often use urgency, fake bank alerts, rewards or shortened links. This site gives an instant first check so you can avoid clicking or sharing sensitive data.
Quick features
• Offline single-file tool (no upload) • Shows triggered words • Color-coded result (Red = Unsafe, Green = Safe)

What we look for
• Urgent requests for OTP, password, or transfers.
• Promises of money, gifts, lottery or refund.
• Suspicious links (shortened/unknown domains) or QR link warnings.
• Requests to verify account details or to click a link to 'fix' a problem.
Tip
If unsure, do not click the link. Check with the official service or bank using their verified contact channels.
